About Us

Who We Are

The Williams Economics Society is a student-run organization committed to fostering a deep understanding and appreciation of economics among students at Williams College. We organize various events, workshops, and programs to supplement existing academic opportunities and provide practical insights into the field of economics.

Our mission is to create a vibrant community of economics enthusiasts, facilitate impactful engagement, and prepare students for successful careers in economics and related fields.
